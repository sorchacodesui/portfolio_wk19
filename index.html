About Me

With my foundations in customer service and user research at the Minneapolis Institute of Art, I've come to the UX field with a broad skillset and a mission to center users in all that I do. I'm driven by a desire to make digital experiences of public services, like government administration, transit, and education more equitable and accessible.

These are the everday services that we all interact with, and yet they're often arduous, complex, and frustrating experiences. More importantly, when the challenges of navigating these services becomes great, they become barriers to people accessing their basic needs. My approach to UX is to take aim at these experiences to make them the public goods we all need them to be.

My knowledge of web development, design-thinking, and quantitative and qualitative analytics makse me uniquely equipped to take on UX challenges of this kind.
